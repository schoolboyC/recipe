Step 1: Get all of your ingredients together

Now what you want to do is get these ingredients to start to cook this dish

This is the "C way" of making a grilled cheese

  • Bread or toast
  • sliced cheese
  • butter
  • choice of meat

Note:

Me personally, I like to add ham or these sauages to the grilled cheese for a better taste.Do not feel pressured to make it this way if you do not like your grilled cheese like this.

Before you get started cooking

To start, first you turn the stove on to a medium temperature and then place a little bit of butter on to the stove and let it melt.Once the butter has melted the first time you then want to add another small amount of butter and quickly add your texas toast on the skillet.
